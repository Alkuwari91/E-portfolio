Unit 1: Introduction to Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning (ML) has become an integral part of modern life, influencing everyday activities from internet browsing to complex decision-making systems in finance. ML enables machines to learn from data and make decisions autonomously. With the growing availability of real-time big data, machine learning algorithms are becoming increasingly sophisticated, leading to more accurate predictions and decisions (Schwab & Zahid, 2020).

This week you will learn about:

  • The past, present, and future of machine learning.
  • The challenges and opportunities of using machine learning algorithms in various industries.
  • The interplay between big data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

On completion of this week, you will be able to:

  • Understand the evolving role of machine learning in future industries.
  • Identify the skill sets required to become proficient in machine learning.
  • Recognize the potential pitfalls of machine learning and explore ways to address them.
