Unit 1: Introduction to Machine Learning (ML)

Welcome to Week 1.

Machine learning (ML) has become an integral part of our daily lives. From browsing the internet to getting a bank loan, ML is used for making decisions and profiling. The availability of real-time big data facilitates the training of ML algorithms, enabling machines to make increasingly accurate decisions autonomously (Schwab & Zahid 2020).

This week, we shall cover:

  • The past, present, and future of machine learning.
  • Challenges and opportunities in using algorithms.
  • The interplay between big data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Understand the role of machine learning in future industries.
  • Identify the skill sets needed to become proficient in machine learning.
  • Recognize the pitfalls of machine learning and approaches to address them.
