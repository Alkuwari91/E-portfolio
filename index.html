Unit 1: Introduction to Machine Learning (ML)

Welcome to Week 1.

Machine learning has become an integral part of our daily life. From browsing on the internet to getting a bank loan, machine learning (ML) is used to make decisions and for profiling. The availability of big data on a real-time basis facilitates the learning of ML algorithms, enabling machines to autonomously make more accurate decisions (Schwab & Zahid, 2020).

This week you will learn about:

  • The past, present, and future of machine learning.
  • Challenges and opportunities in using algorithms.
  • Interplay among big data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

On completion of this week, you will be able to:

  • Understand the role of machine learning in future industries.
  • Identify the skill sets required to become proficient in machine learning.
  • Recognize the pitfalls of machine learning and ways to address them.
